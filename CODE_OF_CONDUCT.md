
# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, respectful and productive space for everyone who wants to use, report issues, suggest improvements or contribute code.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and patient with other users and maintainers.
- Give clear feedback and assume good intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project, its ecosystem and its users.
- Report bugs with enough context to reproduce them.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, threats or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling or intentionally derailing issues and pull requests.
- Pressuring maintainers for unpaid immediate support.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide or remove comments, close issues, block users, or take any other moderation action needed to keep the project healthy.

## Reporting

Report conduct problems privately to `imperazim2@gmail.com`. Include links, screenshots or context when possible.
